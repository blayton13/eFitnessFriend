About Us

Obesity is one of the most significant factors for type 2 diabetes and cardiovascular disease. iFitnessFriend realized it is an issue that can be resolved through well-catered meals and routine exercises. Fortunately, there are many solutions to today's obesity issue, not many answers; however, cater to people with underlying health issues that could prevent them from working out altogether. iFitnessFriend is a weight-watching application that is dedicated to helping individuals with depression, insomnia, metabolic syndrome, and other underlying conditions. Get iFitnessFriend now, and let's get healthier together.

We are driven to help others become and stay healthy. At an affordable price!
